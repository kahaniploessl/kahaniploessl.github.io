Kahani Ploessl
is an interdisciplinary artist fascinated by the realms between physical and digital, natural and technological. Endeavouring to imagine what comes next in the human chapter, Kahani’s work explores new experiences in emerging hybrid spaces, either through inventive prototypes or imaginative illustrations, paintings, and sculpture.

As a mixed race artist raised closely by her Indian immigrant elders, much of Kahani's work examines posthuman indentities by centering South Asian culture and drawing parallels between Hinduism and technological transformation.
