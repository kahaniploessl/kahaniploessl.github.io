Collective Ātma

Web Version (Requires PS4 Dualshock): Play Now!

Kinky Love - Khyber, Halifax, August 1-17 2024

“ātma in virtual avatārs” is a video game installation that depicts the cosmic soul of avatar bodies, exploring the interplay between physical and virtual spaces and the energy between two entities. Intended to be experienced by two people, players interact with the installation by sharing a controller; each half of the controller represents different contributions to the ephemeral and collective aura.
