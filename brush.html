BRUSH.jpeg: Draw Here!

Code Editor: View Code Here!

Coded Brushes Workshop Slide Deck, Centre For Arts Tapes, June 19 2025: View Here!



BRUSH.jpeg is a coded drawing interface that replaces conventional brushes with digital artifacts of the World Wide Web—uploaded photos, webcam captures, or API-generated images become the painterly materials of the artist. Smeared strokes of images dragged across the screen, construct fragmented compositions of drawing, collage, and software art.
