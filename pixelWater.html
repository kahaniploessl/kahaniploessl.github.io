pixelWater

Website: Play Here!


pixelWater is an interactive webpage exploring the digital immortalization of fleeting moments and the fluidity of internet spaces.

Every moment is one of a kind and yet we capture their picture, their sound, their essence, so we may have more time. pixelWater digitally mimics experiences with water so as to recall our sense of play and exploration in the ephemeral. You are welcome to test the water - to swish, splash, glide, and click - to generate moments inimitable.

Exploring the permeability of internet spaces, pixelWater collages fragmented reproductions of water via open sourced APIs (Freesound and Pexels). Randomly pulling files from the ocean of digitized content, pixelWater imagines internet spaces as an ecosystem, meant to evolve and synthesize into beautifully coded experiences.

Similarly to capturing fleeting moments and creating digital artifacts, pixelWater invites itself to be downloaded. Clicking the web page downloads pixelWater’s latest frame. Website: https://pixelwater.glitch.me
