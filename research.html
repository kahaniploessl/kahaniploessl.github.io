Bodies in Play

https://bodiesinplay.org


Research Assistant
Game:Play Lab, Social Body Lab, OCAD U
2023 - 2024


Bodies in Play (BiP) is a three-year collaboration between OCAD University’s game:play Lab and Social Body Lab and Dames Making Games (DMG), aiming to revolutionize creative technology by prioritizing inclusive and innovative design practices. Through game jams and a residency program, we’re bridging the gap between creative, technical, and conceptual work in wearable technology and extended reality (XR).

De-generate

https://kahaniploessl.github.io


Undergraduate Thesis Project
Digital Futures, OCAD U
2022 - 2023


De-generate is an experimental fighting video game that explores how generative art may permeate as not only a technological aesthetic, but an essential component in gameplay and game evolution. Primarily realized through generative characters, digital beings and their core structural code may be intercepted and facilitated by the player, resulting in new, adaptive, and expressive experiences.
